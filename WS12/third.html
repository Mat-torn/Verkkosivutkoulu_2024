BBA student at Laurea University of applied sciences. Qualification in software development.

My story

I've done 2D and 3D art since I was 13 and I always knew I wanted to put these skills to use in my future career. My hobbies include all kinds of art forms such as painting, sketching and guitar playing.

As an artist, I want to offer art for business use and anyone who needs visual representation in their projects. I value good quality art and it's important to me to produce original content.

I want to fight for the right of artist and to make a stop for AI art.
